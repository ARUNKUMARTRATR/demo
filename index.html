WHO AM I??

I’m Vishnu M, a SEBI Registered Investment Adviser. My journey in finance began with trading, evolved into long-term investing, and ultimately led me into advisory — where I combine real-world market experience with disciplined, goal-based planning.

“Finance for Life”

My mission is to help you manage your finances with clarity, structure, and disciplined strategy.

Money is not the goal — it is a powerful tool. When managed wisely, it creates freedom, security, and peace of mind. Through thoughtful, goal-based financial planning, I strive to create meaningful impact in the lives of the individuals and families I serve.

My responsibility is simple — to help you enjoy today with confidence while building a secure and purposeful tomorrow.

HOW IT WORKS?

A disciplined, structured process designed to help you move from financial uncertainty to long-term clarity.

1️⃣ Discover

Understand your current financial position, responsibilities, and life goals.

2️⃣ Design

Build a structured, goal-based financial roadmap aligned with your risk profile.

3️⃣ Execute

4️⃣ Monitor
